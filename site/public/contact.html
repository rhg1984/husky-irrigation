Before you reach out.

How fast do you respond?

expand_more

Most email and text inquiries get a reply within a few hours during business hours, and always within 24 hours. On-site quote appointments are usually scheduled within 2–3 business days.

Do you charge for quotes?

expand_more

No. On-site measurements and written quotes are always free. You'll get a detailed proposal with zone layout and itemized pricing via email within 48 hours of the visit.

What information should I include in my message?

expand_more

Address, approximate lot size if you know it, and a short description of what you're hoping for (full-yard system, drip for beds, smart controller upgrade, etc.). A photo or two of the yard helps us give a rough ballpark before we visit.

Do you service my suburb?

expand_more

We regularly serve Dallas proper and most of the north and east suburbs — Plano, Richardson, Frisco, Garland, Rowlett, Mesquite, Irving, Highland Park, University Park, Lakewood, and Preston Hollow. If you're further out, text us the address and we'll let you know.

Can you work around my landscaping or existing fence?

expand_more

Yes. We hand-dig trenches near mature trees and beds to protect roots, and we route around fences, patios, and hardscape without removing anything. If we see something that can't be avoided, we'll walk you through it before we touch it.

What's the next step after I submit the form?

expand_more

We'll reply to confirm, schedule a 30–60 minute on-site visit, and email you a written quote within 48 hours after. If you say go, we file the city permit (typically 2–5 business days) and schedule the install — most yards are done in 1–3 days.
