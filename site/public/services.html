Your Installation Timeline

1

The Digital Estimate

We use satellite imagery and your property's water pressure data to provide a detailed line-item quote within 24 hours.

2

Permit & Markings

We secure city permits and call 811 to mark utility lines. Our designer flags every head location for your approval.

3

Trench & Install

Our crew arrives, trenches the runs, and installs the pipe in the trenches with careful hand-work to keep your lawn intact. Most residential installs finish in 1–2 days.

4

Final Walkthrough

We program your controller, teach you how to use the app, and adjust every head for perfect, wind-resistant coverage.

Frequently Asked

How long does an installation typically take? expand_more

Most standard residential lots in the Dallas area are completed within 1 to 3 days, depending on soil composition and the number of zones.

Will my grass be ruined after digging? expand_more

We use a trencher and careful hand-digging designed to minimize disruption. Within 2 weeks of regular watering, you won't even see the trench lines.

Do I need to be home for the installation? expand_more

We only need access to your garage for the controller installation and your water main. We can coordinate key access if you're working.
