Frequently Asked

How long does an installation typically take? expand_more

Most standard residential lots in the Dallas area are completed within 1 to 3 days, depending on soil composition and the number of zones.

Will my grass be ruined after digging? expand_more

We use a trencher and careful hand-digging designed to minimize disruption. Within 2 weeks of regular watering, you won't even see the trench lines.

Do I need to be home for the installation? expand_more

We only need access to your garage for the controller installation and your water main. We can coordinate key access if you're working.
