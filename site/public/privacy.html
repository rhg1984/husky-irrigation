Privacy Policy

How Husky Irrigation collects, uses, and protects personal information from quote requests and website visits.

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Husky Irrigation ("Husky," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information from visitors to our website and customers who request quotes or services. Husky Irrigation is a family-owned irrigation contractor based in Garland, Texas, operating under Texas Irrigator License LI0022106.

Who we are

Husky Irrigation is a Dallas-area sprinkler installation business owned and operated by Rigo Gonzalez. Our mailing address is 909 Sara Dunn Drive, Garland, TX 75042. You can reach us at info@HuskyIrrigationTX.com or 469-332-2230. We serve homeowners and small commercial properties in Dallas, Plano, Richardson, Highland Park, Garland, Rowlett, Carrollton, and surrounding North Texas suburbs.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

When you request a quote, schedule an estimate, send us a text message, or contact us by email, we collect the information you choose to share with us. This typically includes:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address (the property where work would be performed)
  • Photographs of your yard, existing system, or relevant property features that you send to help us prepare a quote
  • Any details you provide about your project, budget, timeline, or property

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we and our hosting provider may automatically collect basic technical information, including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring website, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used to operate the site, diagnose problems, and understand how visitors use our content.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests and prepare written estimates
  • Schedule site visits and installations
  • Communicate with you about your project, including text messages, emails, and phone calls about scheduling, materials, permits, and invoicing
  • Apply for permits with the City of Dallas or other local jurisdictions on your behalf
  • Issue invoices and process payment
  • Send occasional follow-up messages about completed work, warranty matters, or seasonal reminders (such as winterization)
  • Comply with legal, tax, licensing, and insurance obligations
  • Improve our website and services

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the limited circumstances described below:

  • Service providers. We use a small set of vendors to operate our business — for example, our website host, email provider, SMS provider, and accounting software. These vendors only receive the information they need to perform their service for us.
  • Permit applications. When we apply for an irrigation permit with the City of Dallas or another local jurisdiction, we provide the property address and the property owner's name as required by the permit application. We do not share your phone number or email with permitting authorities unless they require it.
  • Occasional subcontractors. Husky is a small crew and we self-perform every install, but in rare cases we may engage a licensed subcontractor (for example, an electrician for a dedicated controller circuit). When we do, we share only the property address and the scope of the work, never your contact information without your permission.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect our rights, property, or the safety of others.
  • Business transfer. If Husky Irrigation is sold or merged, customer information may transfer to the successor business, subject to this policy.

Text messages and SMS consent

If you text us — for example, by clicking a "Text Us" link on our site or by sending a message to 469-332-2230 — you consent to receive text replies from us about your inquiry, your project, scheduling, and warranty matters. Message and data rates may apply. You can stop receiving texts at any time by replying STOP, and you can request help by replying HELP. We will not use your phone number for marketing campaigns or share it with third-party marketers.

Cookies and analytics

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and to understand site traffic. We do not run third-party advertising trackers or ad-retargeting pixels on our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; doing so will not prevent you from using the site.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we hold. Our website is served over HTTPS, our email and SMS providers use industry-standard encryption in transit, and access to customer records is limited to the people on our crew who need it. No system is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

Data retention

We keep customer records — quote details, invoices, permit records, and warranty documentation — for as long as we are legally or operationally required to. Tax records and permit records are typically retained for at least seven years. Quote requests that do not become projects are typically purged within twelve months.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records (for example, tax and permit records)
  • Opt out of any non-transactional communication at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@HuskyIrrigationTX.com. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Our services are directed at property owners and adult decision-makers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be highlighted on this page for at least 30 days. Your continued use of our website or services after a change indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
