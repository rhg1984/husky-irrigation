Our story

We do one thing — new installations — and we do it right.

Husky Irrigation is family-owned, Dallas-based, and built on a simple premise: master one service, stand behind it, and stay small enough to walk every yard ourselves.

Rigo spent a decade as a crew lead for larger DFW irrigation outfits before he struck out on his own in 2017. He'd seen too many corners cut — undersized mains, lazy head spacing, controllers shoved in garages where nobody could reach them — and decided if his name was on the job, it would be done the way he'd want it done on his own house.

We're deliberately small. Husky is Rigo, his brother, and a two-person crew he's worked with for years. No rotating subcontractors, no sales team, no pressure. The person who quotes your yard is the person who installs it.

We only do new installations. Not repairs, not service calls for other companies' systems — new systems only. That focus is what lets us dial in the details: pipe sizing for Dallas water pressure, head placement that actually accounts for prevailing wind, and controllers mounted where you'll use them.

Dallas clay and Texas heat are unforgiving. A system installed without thought will drown half your lawn and starve the other half within two summers. We obsess over head-to-head coverage, matched precipitation rates, and drip for anything that isn't turf — because a sprinkler system should last a decade, not a warranty window.

Our promise: no high-pressure sales, no upsells you didn't ask about, no corner-cutting. We quote the job, pull the permit, do the work, and come back in year one for any adjustments at no charge.
