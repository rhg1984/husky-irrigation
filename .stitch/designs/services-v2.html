Common Questions

How long does a typical installation take?

Most residential yards in Dallas are completed within 1 to 3 days, from the first trench to the final sod restoration.

Do you handle all the city permits?

Yes. As a licensed irrigator, Rigo Gonzalez handles the entire permit process with your municipality, including required backflow testing.

What brands do you use?

We exclusively use professional-grade components from Hunter, Rain Bird, and Rachio. No big-box retail components.

Will the installation damage my existing grass?

We take pride in our "invisible" installs. We hand-cut sod and hand-dig trenches to ensure your lawn looks pristine just days after we leave.

Do you offer financing?

We provide flexible payment terms for full system installations. Contact us for our current landscape financing partners.

What does the 2-year warranty cover?

It covers 100% of parts and labor for any manufacturer defects or installation issues. We stand behind our precision work.
